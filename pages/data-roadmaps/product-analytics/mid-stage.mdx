# Mid Stage

At the mid-stage of a company, the focus is on scaling your product and growing your customer base.
You have a product that is working, and you are looking to expand your reach and increase your revenue.

## Key Challenges

The core theme of this stage is growth. The key challenges at this stage are:

- Continue to acquire new customers
- Increase the engagement of your existing customers
- Optimize your product to drive more revenue
- Deliver value to your customers to retain them

## Analytics Goals

Your product analytics efforts at this stage should focus on:

### Feature Adoption and Usage

### NPS and Customer Satisfaction

### Customer Lifetime Value
