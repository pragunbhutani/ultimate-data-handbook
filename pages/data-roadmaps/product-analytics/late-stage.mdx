# Late Stage

At the late stage of a company, the focus is on optimizing your product and maximizing the value you deliver to your customers
and as a result, increase the lifetime value of your customers.

## Key Challenges

The key challenges at this stage are:

- Maximizing the lifetime value of your customers by delivering more value
- Reducing churn and retaining your customers
- Increasing customer satisfaction and loyalty
- Experimenting with new features and products to differentiate and diversify your offerings
